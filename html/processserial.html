Upload Through Serial


  1. Select the serial port you would like to transfer through.
  2. Select the baudrate at which you would like to transfer.
  3. Press continue to proceed to the upload page.
  4. Open IO Ninja.
  5. Create a new serial connection to your device at the same baudrate as you have selected.
  6. Click "File", "Browse" to the desired firmware file and press "Send".
